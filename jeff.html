Welcome! I'm a graphic design student in London Ontario and I'll be assembling this website as a design portfolio over the next two years alongside my studies. In the meantime, it will be home to some of my synth programing and various sound design beeps.

 

***

 

Before entering design, I studied radio broadcasting and have a bunch of experience on-air hosting and off-air producing. From slick commercial to freeform avant-garde, I love the medium.

 

I've also got about half of a Psychology undergrad and a writing minor under my belt from UWO... that I just might wrap up once these graphics start getting churned out.

 

Ultimately, I'm interested in communicating ideas to an audience and making sure they stick.
