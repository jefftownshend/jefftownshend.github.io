The Roland TR-330 is a warm little box. It's kick is full, it's snare punchy and the hats are classic.

 

Only playable with it's built in rhythms, these samples have been chopped out of a recording made from the direct line out in the back.

 

The original raw recordings are available along with an EQed set I prepared with some noise cleaned up, some frequencies boosted and the lowend of the kick cut for layering purposes.

 

These samples are hard to find and I'm fairly confident I've got the web's finest collection right here for free.
