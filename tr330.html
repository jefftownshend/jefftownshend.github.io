The Roland TR-330 is a warm little box. It's kick is full and it's snare punchy.

 

These samples have been chopped out of a series of pattern recordings made from it's rear output.

 

The original raw hits are available along with an EQed set I prepared with some noise cleaned up, some frequencies boosted and the low-end of the kick cut for potential layering.

 

It's a hard to find set and I'm fairly confident I've got the web's finest collection right here, for free.

 

Link to Zip containing 2 sets of kicks, snares, closed & open hats, claves, blocks, high congas and low congas.
