The Roland TR-330 is Japanese made rhythm machine from 1973. It's kick is full, it's snare is punchy and it's relation to the CR-78 and TR-808 is apparent.

 

These files have been sampled out of a series of pattern recordings from it's rear output and not recorded from it's
unique built in speaker.

 

The original raw hits are available here along with an EQed set I've prepared with some noise cleaned up, some frequencies boosted and the low-end of the kick cut for potential layering.

 

Inconveniently, the sounds have been packaged as wavefiles containing multiple takes of each hit. This allows for superior "round-robin" sample playback, but requires some trimming on your part.

 

Link to Zip containing 2 sets of kicks, snares, closed & open hats, claves, blocks, high congas and low congas.
