∆ The Century of the Self (2002)

Youtube (4 hours in 3 parts)

Adam Curtis explains a wide range of topics related to the origins of modern advertising and public relations. Not graphic design focused but a fascinating introduction to the overlap between many fields including Freudian psychology, demographics and government.
