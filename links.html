I'm going to use this page to gather information and take notes on topics which I think might be interesting to fellow graphic design students.

 

Please note that I'm not endorsing these works. Edward Bernay's writing for example explores ethically controversial topics but is worth reading as a historical document.

 

The Century of the Self

Youtube (4 hours in 3 parts)

Adam Curtis explains a wide range topics related to the origins of modern advertising and public relations. Not graphic design focused but a fascinating introduction to the overlap between many fields including Freudian psychology, manipulation and government.

 

Propaganda by Edward Bernays

Full book - Archive

Featured in The Century of the Self, this is seen as the birth of public relations. Worth an exploration and still resonates today in modern advertising.
